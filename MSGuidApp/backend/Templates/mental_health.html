Mental Health Support for People with MS

Why Mental Health Matters with MS

Living with Multiple Sclerosis can be emotionally challenging. Stress, anxiety, depression, and fatigue are common. Getting support for your mental health is just as important as managing physical symptoms.

Resources Available

  • National MS Society: Offers free counseling referrals and peer support programs.
  • Counseling Services: Access licensed therapists who specialize in chronic illness.
  • Mindfulness Exercises: Guided meditations and breathing techniques to reduce stress.
  • Support Groups: Join online communities where people share their experiences and coping strategies.
  • Crisis Hotlines: For urgent emotional support or distress.

How to Get Help

You can reach out through:

  • Your neurologist or primary care provider
  • The National MS Society Helpline: Visit Website
  • Therapy platforms like BetterHelp or Talkspace
  • Local mental health clinics or telemedicine services
